Frequently asked questions

How long does it take?

Most Wash & Fold orders are returned in ~24 hours. Dry cleaning typically takes ~24–48 hours. Same-day may be available with early pickups.

What happens with stains or delicate items?

We pretreat visible stains and follow care labels. For delicates or special care, choose Dry Cleaning or leave a note at checkout.

How do you track orders?

Every bag receives a scan ID with item counts/weights. You’ll get text updates from pickup to delivery.

Is there a minimum?

Some areas have per-order minimums or different same-day rules. Full details appear in the scheduler for your ZIP.

What if I’m not home?

No problem—set a safe drop-off spot in your instructions. We’ll confirm with a photo at delivery.
